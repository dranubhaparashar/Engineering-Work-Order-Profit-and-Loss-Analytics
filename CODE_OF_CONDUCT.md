# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Contributors are expected to maintain a respectful, inclusive, professional, and harassment-free environment.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate constructively.
- Respect differing experience levels and viewpoints.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Protect privacy and confidential information.
- Use synthetic examples in public discussions.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, publication of private information, or deliberately disruptive behavior is not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions and may restrict participation when this code is violated. Serious concerns should be reported privately to the repository owner.
